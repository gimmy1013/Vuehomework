ABOUT FRAGRANCE LASTING
The fragrance in "PONG" products is carefully formulated to last between 4 to 6 weeks, providing an enduring and delightful aroma that lingers in your space, creating a captivating and immersive sensory experience.
ABOUT PAYMENT METHODS
You can pay for orders on our online website using major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment gateways. We ensure safe and encrypted transactions for a seamless shopping experience.
ABOUT DELIVERY TIMEFRAME
Delivery timeframes for our online website vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen. Typically, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days for domestic shipments.
ABOUT OUR COLLABORATION
Our exclusive collaborations with the esteemed organic brand, Florihana, bring our reed diffusers to a new level. Infused with their premium organic essential oils, our products offer an unparalleled sensory journey.
ABOUT ENVIRONMENTAL CARE
Our diffusers are crafted using eco-friendly materials, and we promote sustainability by using natural ingredients and recyclable packaging, ensuring a green and delightful aromatic experience for all.
